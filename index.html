Brand story

OnTik – Indigenous street and training wear

OnTik is my clothing brand about how we identify ourselves – who we are as Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander mob, who we walk with and where we come from. It’s about culture, staying grounded and not forgetting who we are – the stories and songlines that follow us wherever we go.

Too often our people are wearing big overseas brands that don’t know our Country, our colours or our story. OnTik flips that – bringing Indigenous-led design and story into streetwear and training gear that actually fits our lifestyles.

About Nathan

Nathan “Belly” – OnTik.wear

Created by a local Arrernte man from Central Australia, OnTik is built from years of working with community and young mob – on ovals, in programs and in everyday life. The same values that drive that work sit inside this brand: respect, culture, strength and showing up.

OnTik isn’t about chasing hype. It’s about giving our people something they can wear with pride – on Country, in town, at the gym or travelling – and knowing it comes from someone who lives the story, not just prints it.
